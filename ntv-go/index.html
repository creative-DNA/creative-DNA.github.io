Overview

About NTV GO

NTV GO enables you to engage and contribute to your favourite content through storytelling. We extend our resources so each of you can participate in journalism and engage with NTV Uganda. Contribute to your favourite shows, news features, and stories — all in one place, with a seamless experience on mobile and web.

Features

Key features

Enjoy stories and events happening in Uganda through informative video content
Contribute to your favourite event or become a reporter — get featured on television and digital properties
Watch Spark TV livestream alongside NTV content
Engage with news and shows in a single, mobile-first experience

Origin

Background

NTV GO was conceived from the NTV Ideaslab, an initiative of Africa Broadcasting (U) Ltd and Outbox Uganda that sought innovative digital ideas to improve how news is collected and disseminated, how media engages audiences, and how new production and revenue models emerge in media.
